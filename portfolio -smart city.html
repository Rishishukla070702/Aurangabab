SMART CITY


AURANGABAD SMART CITY

Aurangabad Smart City Development Corporation Limited (ASCDCL) is a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) registered under Section 8 of the Companies Act, 2013. ASCDCL was incorporated in September 2016, following the selection of Aurangabad in Round 2 of Smart City Mission Challenge, for the implementation of the Smart City project. ASCDCL is jointly owned by Government of Maharashtra and Aurangabad Municipal Corporation. ASCDCL is focused to deliver projects which are sustainable and socially inclusive. With the ongoing project of City Bus Service and upcoming key projects of upgrading the Public Transport by introducing Bus Shelters, E-Ticketing, Vehicle tracking system, Digital outdoor displays; E-Governance, Master System Integrator, Solid Waste Management, development of Aurangabad Safari Park and Conservation of Historical Gates.

SMART PROJECTS

Smart City Bus

A survey along with Citizen engagement program was conducted by Aurangabad Municipal Corporation (AMC) in 2016, to determine key priority areas, for which smart city should be working in, wherein public transport was a second topmost priority in Aurangabad. Keeping this in mind and seeing the non-availability of public transport in Aurangabad, Aurangabad Smart City Development Corporation Limited (ASCDCL) initiated a joint venture with Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTDC). Project was flagged off on 23 rd Jan 2019, inaugurated by hands of Yuva Sena Chief Aditya Thackeray.

Features

  1. Smart City Bus Division along with MSRTC started one of its kind a Public Public Partnership with having commenced its operation on 23rd January 2019 and today operates across city on 27 routes with fleet size of 100 Buses.
  2. Many Commuter centric Projects are undertaken under the Smart City Bus Service to facilitate the citizens.
  3. The corridors and routes have been planned considering the demand and the buses are scheduled in order to have minimum waiting period for the public. With the introduction of VTS and mobile application, accessing the Smart buses would become much more easier and faster for the communters.
  4. This project is committed to provide citizens with the best public transport experience which is efficient, affordable, safe, reliable and comfortable which would enable citizens to make a modal shift from private vehicles to public transport.
  5. Smart city buses are equipped with CCTV cameras, Digital Route Display boards, On-board vehicle diagnostics, Vehicle tracking systems and various other critical safety features.

INTEGRATED MUNICIPAL E-GOVERNANCE SYSTEM

Project Features: In continuation with the implementation of various E-Governance initiatives, AMC has now embarked upon a plan for the design, development and implementation of an Integrated Municipal E-Governance System (I-MEGS). Specifically, AMC seeks to use the proposed E- Governance solution, as the primary delivery channel to provide a single, easy, secure, integrated, and reliable means of access to municipal information and services in order to continuously improve the quality of services provided to residents and businesses in Aurangabad. The mission of the project is to provide a unified information communication technology platform for transparency and participative good governance. This is expected to be a critical instrument for upgrading the quality of the service deliverables across AMC.

SAFARI PARK

AURANGABAD ZOOLOGICAL PARK

Project Features:

  1. Aurangabad Smart City Development Corporation Limited (ASCDCL) planned to develop Aurangabad Zoological Park one of its kind Smart Zoo in country as area based development under Smart City Mission.
  2. Master plan for Aurangabad Zoological Park located at Mitmita, Aurangabad has been approved by Central Zoo Authority, Government of India on 26/02/2020.
  3. Detail drawing and stage wise work estimate and tender preparation is in progress

Project Statistic

Name of Location: Gut No 307, Mitmita, Aurangabad

Project Area : 100 acres

Animals : 500 animals of over 60 odd species approx.

Annual Visitors : 10 lakh (Planned) Project

Budget : 167 crores
