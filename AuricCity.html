AURIC: Aurangabad Industrial City

AURIC is planned with the idea of integrating city infrastructure with information and communication technology. This will optimize the use of resources – land, water and energy, reduce impacts on the environment, and enhance livability - thus making AURIC a globally competitive investment destination.

Shendra IA covers an area of 8.39 sqkm located north of Jalna Road adjoining existing MIDC Shendra Industrial Park

Bidkin IA covers an area of 32.03 sqkm located east of Paithan Road located near Bidkin.

Aurangabad is a city in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Located on a hilly upland terrain in the Deccan Traps, Aurangabad is the fifth-most populous urban area in Maharashtra with a population of 1,175,116. The city is known as a major production center of cotton textile and artistic silk fabrics.
