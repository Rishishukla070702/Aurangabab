Marathwada Environmental Care Cluster

MECC The Company

BRIEF HISTORY OF
(MECC)

In January 2016 Company in the name of Marathwada Environmental Care Cluster (MECC) was Formed under section 8 of Companies Act of India. Which is a Non Profit Company.Mr. B.S. Khose was the Founder Chairman. Mr. Ram Bhogale lead the Company during 2017-18.

While extending the work of the Company in the City of Aurangabad it was launched under the Project Aurangabad First. In 2018 Company got Aurangabad First registered as official Trademark under social platform.

Marathwada Environmental Care Cluster (MECC) is founded by a visionary entrepreneur Mr. B.S. Khose for sustainable development & inclusive growth of this region as well as to help attracting investment. Roots of MECC goes back to 2009 when Mr. B.S Khose started tree plantation in sport authority of India (SAI) & various places in MIDC Waluj by investing own funds to increase green cover of this region. From 2009 to 2012 thousand of trees with proper care for their survival and growth were planted.

01

TREE PLANATION

Tree plant of around 60000 trees on the road side spaces with chain link fencing & legal ownership & responsibility of maintaining the green belt in Waluj MIDC.

COLLECTION OF GARBAGE

Collection of garbage on daily basis from all MIDC (3000Acres) & keeping it clean for 19 (Nineteen) months.

03

GARBAGE TO GAS PLANT

Initiating an idea of garbage to gas plant, preparing the project report, following it up with MIDC, getting it sanctioned, assessing the technology with necessary technical inputs, monitoring its construction & time schedule to commissioning. Today (30/3/2019) construction / erection is complete and plant will be commissioned in April 2019 .This plant is the only one of its kind in any industrial & area in Maharashtra and possibly in India.

PROGRAM ON 26TH JAN 2016

MECC with the help of Bajaj Auto & BAVA arranged a program on 26th Jan 2016 & 26th Jan 2017 to administer the oath of cleanliness & greenery to more than 10000 (Ten Thousand) participants each year from all cross section of the society like industrialist, staff, workers, School children and citizens
